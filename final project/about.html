About WeatherNow

WeatherNow is your reliable, real-time source for local weather updates across Uganda.

Whether you're planning a trip, commuting to work, or just heading out for a walk, we help you stay ahead with accurate temperature and wind speed forecasts.

Our goal is to simplify weather access for everyone, especially Ugandans seeking localized, timely forecasts on mobile devices.
