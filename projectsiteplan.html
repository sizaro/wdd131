Weather App Project Proposal

Overview

Purpose

The Weather App is a basic website that displays weather information for a specific city. The app provides essential weather data such as temperature and wind speed, offering users a quick way to check weather updates. The project is designed to help me practice web development skills, specifically focusing on HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Audience

The target audience for this app includes anyone looking for quick and simple weather updates for a city. The app will cater to users of all ages, from casual web users to those who need real-time weather information for travel or outdoor activities. The app will be accessible on both mobile and desktop devices, with a user-friendly interface for all types of users, including those with disabilities.
